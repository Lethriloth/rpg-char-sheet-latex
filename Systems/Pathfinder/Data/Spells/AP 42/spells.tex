    \DeclareSpellDescription{Constricting Coils}{This spell functions like hold monster, except that the target is constricted as if by the coils of a large snake, taking 1d6+6 points of bludgeoning damage each round that it fails its save. A successful save ends both the paralyzing and constriction effects.}
