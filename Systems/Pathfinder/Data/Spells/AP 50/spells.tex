    
\DeclareSpell{Aspect of the Nightingale}{transmutation (polymorph) []|V,  S,  DF|1 standard action|personal|Targetsyou|1 minute/level||}[]
    \DeclareSpellDescription{Aspect of the Nightingale}{You take on an aspect of a nightingale. Your voice becomes clear and pleasant. You gain a +2 competence bonus on Perform (sing) checks and a +2 competence bonus on Diplomacy checks. Once per minute, if you are subject to a charm effect that allows a saving throw, you may roll twice and take the more favorable result.}
